About Us
Lige Ma LLC is a start-up company located in Baltimore, Maryland. The two co-founders are long-time friends that share a passion for sports and games. The idea for a Persian Gulf Pro League gaming platform came from the lack of such a thing existing in the first place! Our mission is to provide prediction, fantasy, and other game modes, to the iranian soccer league fandom.
